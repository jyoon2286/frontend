About Bakers'

We are a family owned business committed to providing quality products and excellent service. Serving the College Park area since 1954, Bakers' is dedicated to providing breads and coffee for all occasions. We create Edible Masterpieces of cakes. We will create the cake of your dreams for your wedding or special event. Beautiful cakes in every style, from classic to trendy, available in a variety of flavors to suit your taste. We don't just bake breads and cakes. We create them. We pour our hearts into each and every one of them. We mix them with love. Using the freshest and finest ingredients like farm-fresh eggs, butter and cream, fresh fruit, fragrant spices and masterfully blend flavors to create our edible masterpieces.
